Privacy Policy for HangTime
Last updated: June 5, 2026
Ember Rose Tech LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the HangTime mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect
Information You Provide

Account information: When you sign in via Google, Apple, or Microsoft, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from that provider.

Information Collected Automatically

Calendar availability: With your permission, we access your connected calendar to determine your free and busy time slots. We store only free/busy intervals — event titles, descriptions, attendees, and locations are never accessed or stored.
Blackout windows: Time ranges you set as unavailable (e.g. weekday evenings, weekends) are stored to exclude them from availability comparisons.
Device timezone: Your device's IANA timezone (e.g. "America/Chicago") is stored to correctly convert your local blackout times for comparison with friends in other timezones.
Push notification token: If you grant permission, we store a device token to send you notifications about connection requests, accepted connections, and availability matches. You can revoke this at any time in your device settings.
Camera: Used only to scan QR codes for joining groups. No images are stored.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect precise location data.
We do not collect calendar event titles, descriptions, attendees, or locations.
We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. How We Share Your Information
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.
We share limited data only as follows:

With friends you connect with: Your display name, profile photo, and free/busy availability are visible to friends you explicitly connect with. Calendar event details are never shared.
With service providers: We use the following services to operate the App:

Google Firebase / Firestore — database and authentication
Google AdMob — advertising (preview/free tier users)
RevenueCat — subscription management
Expo / EAS — push notification delivery


If required by law: We may disclose information in response to valid legal requests.


4. Data Retention
Your account data is retained as long as your account exists. You may delete your account at any time from the Profile screen, which permanently removes your profile, connections, groups, and availability data from our servers.

5. Children's Privacy
HangTime is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

Access the personal data we hold about you
Correct inaccurate data
Request deletion of your data
Withdraw consent for calendar access at any time via your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Security
We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase Security Rules, server-side validation via Cloud Functions, and encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS). Calendar slot data is stored in a private server-only subcollection that is never accessible from the client.
